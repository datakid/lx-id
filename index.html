14 digits, six meanings

      
Every Egyptian national ID packs birth date, sex, and place of registration into a single number. Here's how the positions break down.

    

    

      

        

          1
          

            
Century digit

            
Digit 1. 2 means born in the 1900s, 3 means born in the 2000s.

          

        

      

      

        

          2
          

            
Birth date

            
Digits 2–7, as YY MM DD. Combined with the century digit this gives the full birth date.

          

        

      

      

        

          3
          

            
Governorate code

            
Digits 8–9. Identifies where the ID was first registered — see the Governorates tab for the full list.

          

        

      

      

        

          4
          

            
Serial number

            
Digits 10–12. A sequential registration number for that birth date and governorate.

          

        

      

      

        

          5
          

            
Gender digit

            
Digit 13. Odd means Male, even means Female.

          

        

      

      

        

          6
          

            
Check digit

            
Digit 14. A verification digit issued with the card; this tool records it but doesn't independently validate it.

        Law 148/2019
        
Egypt's phased retirement age increase

      

      
The standard pension age of 60 steps up gradually until it reaches 65. The age that applies to a given person depends on their birth date.
